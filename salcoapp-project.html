Salcobrand App

Salcobrand_ App E-Commerce and Health Helper

Lead Designer | UX | UI

Salcobrand App has been one of my favorite projects. This app is a native mobile made for the E-Commerce Users of the Drugstore Salcobrand. Also, it is helpful for chronically ill people who need to buy medicine or set alarms to take their medication or alerts if there are only few pills left. It offers support for users with diseases and medicines at home.

  • User Experience Design
  • User Interface Design
  • Mobile Apps

Want to know when to take your pills? We'll be your memory!

After a lot of research, and as every Digital Product, this App was the continued project after having finished the Salcobrand.cl site. This time, our aim was creating journeys for users to take pills, buy medicine, products and also offer a search for stores. User Flow interaction was an first hint of what was next in that moment.
After weeks of iteration with the client, I would begin to draw some architecture information like Wireframes and Prototypes in order to validate more of the hypothesis at previous activities for this product. It was an awesome experience to test them in order to continue with this amazing project.
Since I had previously defined the Salcobrand.cl website, the graphics were based on those guidelines, making the app look like this.
