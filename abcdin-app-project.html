Abcdin App

Abcdin E-Commerce & Stores Catalogue

Lead Designer | UX | UI

Abcdin is one of the most huge retailers in Chile. With more than 100 Stores over the country, Abcdin decide to create a new product in order to make multi channel buy journey for their sells. One of those products was Abcdin App, this one let users to consult the stock and details of their products, add them to a wishlist and look for the most closer stores around them. This project was the first one of their class in the Abcdin Company, simultaiously we develop the Totem one that helps users to buy products in the stores through a touch widescreen and POS integrated device (You can see it in one of my other produts as a designer) In this time, was a product developed by a Scrum methodology, allowing us the time to make a pixel perfect product in a short-medium time.
