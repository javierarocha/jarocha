Enel - Chispers

Chispers_ Sharing Energy

Lead Designer | Brand Designer | UX | UI

Chispers is a Enel App based on sharing energy as a social network. Users download the app and begin to obtain a digital coin called ‘Chispers‘ that would help to charge their phones in different points in the city like universities, busses, malls and more.

  • User Experience Design
  • Mobile Apps
  • Project Management
  • Front End Design

Sharing Energy as a social network? Let's see...

This time, there was a pretty big problem: a continuous project of improvement that didn't really work and a very strange client that saw me changing its user interface and put other excentric ideas into a digital product. Well, that was Chispers, a very colourful project that, besides a lot of Sprints and iterations, has become a very valuable product due to its interface and experience. As always, all of it began with Wireframes and Prototyping.
After months of tesingt everywhere, I could finally define a brand design with the Scrum Team that eventually became the face of this product: Mockups that will be printed in malls, universities and electric busses of the government of the city.
