Enel - Chispers

Chispers_ Sharing Energy

Lead Designer | Brand Designer | UX | UI

Chispers is a Enel App based in Sharing Energy as a social network. Users download the app and begin to optain a digital coin called ·Chispers· that helps then to charge their phones in diferent points in the city like universitys, buses, malls and more.

  • User Experience Design
  • Mobile Apps
  • Project Management
  • Front End Design

Sharing Energy as a social network? Let's see...

This time was a very big problem. A continous improvement project that didn't work and a very strange client that find me to change its user interface and put the other excentric ideas in a digital product. Well, that was Chispers, a very colorfull product that besides a lot of Sprints and Iterations became in a very value product because of its interface and experience and all of it began with Wireframes and Prototyping (As always).
After months and test everywhere, i'd finally defined with the Scrum Team a brand design that eventually became in the face of this product. Mockups that will be printed in Malls, Universities and Goverment Electric Buses.
