Groweed

Groweed - Let's grow up you mind

Co-Founder | Lead Designer | UX | UI | Front End Designer

Groweed App was made for people who need to understand the actual Cannabis status about its legal politics and ways to consume it. The first sigh for this project was an edutated app that brings to users the better and certificated way to learn about all the Cannabis World.

There are a lot of things that makes Groweed an Stronger App, one of them basically turns about its easy way to use and eat content; a redifined and selected content for all kind of users (Even thou Kids). Learn about Marihuana its the best way to consume in it in the countries that allow it, specially in medicinal ways.

GEO Referenced Stores inside the app help users to find the best Growshop, about local and prices. We keep always thinking about the big responsibility that brings the fact to own a Cannabis Digital Product, that's why a professional and multidiciplinary team brough together this idea into real life, in order to make a world less ignorant about it.

As a Co-Founder and Lead Designer of the project i had have the opportinity to learn and put all of my heart in every way that a small company like Groweed brings me. Exclusive expierience full of love and a lot of professionalism experiences in just one App will soon available in AppStore and Playstore.
